Haversine Distance

Calculate great-circle distance between two points on Earth's surface

Use Case: Solely engineering the Hybrid CVRPTWTC Bus Allocation System at BigSMS Pty Ltd. Utilizing Haversine distance calculations to compute accurate Earth-surface distances between bus stops, enabling optimal route planning with capacity constraints, time windows, and travel time optimization for efficient bus fleet management.

Quick Examples:
Distance: 0 km
Haversine Formula:

a = sin²(Δφ/2) + cos(φ₁)·cos(φ₂)·sin²(Δλ/2)

c = 2·atan2(√a, √(1−a))

d = R·c (where R = Earth's radius ≈ 6,371 km)
